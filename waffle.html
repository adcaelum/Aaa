Waffle Recipe

A delicious and sweet recipe for perfect waffles.

Ingredients (for 10 waffles):

  • 1 cup (115 g) flour
  • 4 tbsp (36 g) powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp (30 g) butter
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 cup (200 ml) milk
  • 1 envelope baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Oil for cooking
  • Maple syrup

Steps:

  1. Melt butter in a small saucepan (do not boil).
  2. In a mixing bowl, mix in the flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  3. While mixing, add in the egg, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract (if using).
  4. Batter is ready when smooth.
  5. Preheat your waffle iron and lightly grease it with oil.
  6. Pour batter into the waffle iron and cook according to your ironâ€™s instructions, usually 4-5 minutes until golden and crispy.
  7. Serve hot, drizzled with maple syrup!
